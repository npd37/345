Anay Global Pte. Ltd. is a Singapore-based financial consulting firm that partners with businesses to manage their finance, compliance, and reporting functions with precision and confidence.

Led by Minoo Dhandhania, a qualified Singapore Chartered Accountant (ISCA), Fellow Member of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA, UK), and Accredited Tax Practitioner with the Singapore Chartered Tax Professionals (SCTP), the firm combines international expertise with deep local knowledge of Singapore's regulatory landscape.

With over 15 years of experience across audit, financial planning, and compliance, Anay Global supports both local and multinational businesses in achieving:

  • Financial transparency and efficiency
  • Seamless compliance with Singapore Financial Reporting Standards (SFRS)
  • Sustainable business growth through smart financial strategy
